BANK-GRADE SECURITY

Your assets and data are protected by multiple layers of enterprise-grade security infrastructure.

🔐

End-to-End Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is protected with TLS 1.3 and 256-bit AES encryption. Your financial data is never exposed in transit.

ACTIVE — TLS 1.3 / AES-256
📱

Two-Factor Authentication

Mandatory 2FA using TOTP (Time-based One-Time Passwords) compatible with Google Authenticator, Authy, and hardware security keys (FIDO2/WebAuthn).

ACTIVE — TOTP + FIDO2
🎣

Anti-Phishing Protection

Personalized anti-phishing codes on all emails, domain-locked sessions, and real-time phishing URL detection powered by our threat intelligence network.

ACTIVE — THREAT INTEL FEED
👁️

Account Activity Monitoring

24/7 behavioral analytics detect anomalous login patterns. Instant alerts for new device logins, unusual locations, and suspicious transaction patterns.

MONITORING — 24/7 ACTIVE
🚧

Rate Limiting & Brute Force Protection

Intelligent rate limiting blocks brute force attacks after 5 failed attempts. Progressive lockout with CAPTCHA challenge and IP-based banning for repeat offenders.

ACTIVE — 5 ATTEMPT LIMIT
💉

XSS & SQL Injection Prevention

All user inputs are sanitized and parameterized queries prevent SQL injection. Content Security Policy (CSP) headers block cross-site scripting at the browser level.

ACTIVE — CSP ENFORCED
🔑

Secure Password Hashing

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with cost factor 12. Salt rounds prevent rainbow table attacks. Passwords are never stored or logged in plain text.

ACTIVE — BCRYPT CF-12
🍪

Secure Session Management

HttpOnly and SameSite=Strict cookies prevent session hijacking. Sessions expire after 30 minutes of inactivity with automatic re-authentication required.

ACTIVE — SAMESITE STRICT

Frequently Asked Questions

Navigate to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator or Authy. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm. 2FA is required for all withdrawals by default.

Maker fees are 0.08% and taker fees are 0.10%. High-volume traders receive discounts down to 0.01%. There are no deposit fees and crypto withdrawal fees vary by network.

Crypto withdrawals are processed within 30 minutes after security confirmation. Bank transfers take 1-3 business days depending on your bank and jurisdiction.

95% of assets are stored in cold storage. Our hot wallet is covered by a $100M insurance policy. NEXUS maintains a Secure Asset Fund for Users (SAFU) worth $500M.

Immediately change your password, revoke all active sessions in Settings → Security → Active Sessions, and contact our security team at security@nexustrade.io. Enable 2FA if not already active.
